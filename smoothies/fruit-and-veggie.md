# Mike's Classic Fruit and Veggie Smoothie

## Ingredients

### Liquids

* 1/2 cup Almond Milk or Coconut Milk
* 1/2 cup Orange Juice
* 1/2 cup Apple Juice

### Veggies

* Handful of Spinach
* Handful of Kale
* 1/2 Cucumber, sliced
* Broccoli (optional)
* Carrots

### Fruit

* Blueberries
* Strawberries
* 1 Banana
* 1 Apple
* Pineapple slices
* Mango or Papaya chunks

### Spices

* Cinnamon
* Garlic

### Options

* Pomegranate Tea
